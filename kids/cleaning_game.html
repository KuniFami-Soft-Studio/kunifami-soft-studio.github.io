This "Cleaning Game" is designed based on cognitive psychology principles, specifically supporting "Object Permanence" and visuomotor integration. The process of rubbing away the brown grime to reveal hidden items stimulates curiosity and reinforces the concept that objects continue to exist even when unseen. Effectively navigating the screen to clear the canvas builds a foundation for spatial awareness. Visual and haptic feedback ("Sparkle!" message and vibration) trigger the brain's reward system, fostering self-esteem and encouraging the "sustained attention" necessary to complete a task. It is a powerful educational tool for building successful experiences.

From a digital therapy perspective, this app aims to promote "brain activation" and maintain "finger dexterity" for seniors. Rubbing the screen of a tablet or smartphone stimulates the prefrontal cortex and improves hand-eye coordination. The intuitive interface, free of complex rules, reduces resistance to digital devices and allows users to enjoy dementia prevention. Daily "cleaning" sessions serve as a beneficial habit for protecting brain health.
