This "Memory Grid" focuses on enhancing "Working Memory" and "Visuospatial Cognition," critical components of early childhood development. The process of scanning grid layouts within a time limit and mentally maintaining that information while searching for a target stimulates the prefrontal cortex. Adaptive scaffolding through various grid sizes (up to 7x7) encourages persistence and builds self-esteem through immediate feedback. The bilingual interface also supports secondary language acquisition.
