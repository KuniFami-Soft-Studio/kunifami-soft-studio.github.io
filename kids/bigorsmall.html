This application is designed to nurture visual discrimination skills and the foundations of spatial awareness, which are crucial in early childhood cognitive development. The process of connecting abstract concepts like "Big" and "Small" with verbal cues and visual data stimulates logical thinking. By using familiar emojis and randomly alternating the target, the app maintains the child's selective attention. The visual "Star" reward upon a correct answer promotes a sense of achievement and self-esteem, fostering a positive attitude toward learning and foundational mathematical logic (comparison and seriation).
