3 Educational Benefits of This Game

Unlike regular jigsaw puzzles, slide puzzles require thinking about "order."

1. Logical Thinking

"To move this here, I need to move that first..."
Planning steps backward from the goal is the foundation of logical and programming thinking.

2. Spatial Awareness

Kids learn to grasp the position of a fragment within the whole picture.
It's advanced brain training to keep the whole image in mind while moving pieces.

3. Perseverance and Achievement

You can't solve it by moving randomly.
Trial and error and not giving up lead to a great sense of accomplishment when finished.
