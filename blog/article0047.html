Technical Hurdle: iOS Restrictions

In fact, iPhone browsers (Safari) have specifications that "restrict auto-play and simultaneous playback of multiple videos" to save battery and data.
If you simply create a web page, playing one video will automatically pause the other.

To bypass this and sync two videos frame-by-frame, advanced programming (DOM manipulation and timing control via JavaScript) is required.
