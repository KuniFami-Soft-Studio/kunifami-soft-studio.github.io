[Developer Story] Why Are These Professional Tools Free? 3 Reasons Behind Our Mission

2026.02.08 | Lifehack & Tech

"Are these really free?"
"Is there a paid version?"

I often get these questions. Why do I offer high-functioning tools like "Form Mirror" (which could easily be a paid subscription service) for free?

Today, I'd like to share the "3 Wishes" I put into developing these tools.

1. Reducing the Burden on Parents

As a parent, I know that children's lessons (dance, sports, music) cost a lot of money beyond just tuition.

When a child wants to improve, I don't want the cost of expensive apps to be another burden on the family budget.
"I want them to use the best practice tools for free."
This is my primary motivation.

2. Technology for Everyone

Modern devices are powerful, but not everyone has an environment that fully utilizes them.

I want to create a world where "anyone can improve efficiently just by mastering the device they already have," rather than only those who buy expensive software succeeding.
I hope my tools can be "one of the options" to help you achieve that.

3. Joy of Improvement for Adults too

Busy adults also deserve the joy of saying, "I did better than yesterday!" in their hobbies. This sense of achievement brings positivity to daily life.

Please use the time saved by these efficient tools for "praising yourself" or "your next challenge."
